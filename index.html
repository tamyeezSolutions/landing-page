About Us

Markaz Tamyeez is dedicated to providing quality education in Islamic knowledge and the Arabic language. Our mission is to make learning Islam directly from the scholars feasible for all with Allah's permission.


There are many who don't have time, especially in the western lands, because of the large amount of time needed to take care of your worldly affairs. It is not easy to align our schedules with that of instructors. We have the solution so you no longer need to worry about your time constraints. We offer one-on-one live classes as well as pre-recorded so you could study on your own time and still fulfill your religious and worldly obligations.
